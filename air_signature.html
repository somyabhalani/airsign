How to Use AirSign

Step 1: Allow Camera Access

Click "Allow Camera & Begin" to grant AirSign permission to access your webcam. Your camera feed is only used for hand detection and is never stored.

Step 2: Fill Your Details

Enter your:

  • Full Name: Your legal name as it appears on documents
  • Email: Valid email address for record-keeping
  • Company (Optional): Your organization name
  • Date: Automatically set to today (cannot be changed)

Step 3: Read & Agree to Terms

Read the Terms of Agreement carefully and check the checkbox to confirm you agree.

Step 4: Sign Your Document

Left Panel (Camera):

  • Click "Start Signature" to begin signing
  • Raise your index finger (☝️) or pinch your thumb & index to draw
  • Make a fist (✊) to pause drawing temporarily
  • Click "Pause" button to stop, "Resume" to continue
  • Open both hands (🖐️) to clear and start over

Step 5: Capture Your Signature

When satisfied with your signature, click "✓ Use Signature" to capture it.

Step 6: Submit & Download

  • Click "Submit Signed Document" to complete
  • Choose download option:
    • 📥 Download Full PDF: Complete document with all details
    • ✍️ Signature Only: Just your signature image (no text)

Step 7: Sign Another

After downloading, click "↻ Sign Another" to sign a new document.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use good lighting and clear background
  • Keep your hand steady and within camera frame
  • Use smooth, deliberate movements
  • Practice makes perfect - refine your digital signature
  • Save your PDFs securely for legal records

Troubleshooting

  • Camera not detected? Check browser permissions and camera connectivity
  • Hand not detected? Ensure good lighting and keep hand in frame
  • Signature skipping? Move your finger slower or use steady pinching motion
  • Download not working? Check your browser's download settings
